## üì± Real-Time Coordination Signals

### Hand Signals (for non-verbal communication)

- **Tap wrist** = Hurry up, we're over time
- **Thumbs up** = Good job, keep going
- **Flat hand wave** = Slow down, too fast
- **Point to self** = I'll take this question
- **Cut throat** = Skip this, move to next

### Verbal Signals

- **"As [Name] will show..."** = You're up next, get ready
- **"Let me quickly show..."** = I'm wrapping up this section
- **"Building on that..."** = Reinforcing previous speaker
- **"To recap..."** = Signaling transition to next person
